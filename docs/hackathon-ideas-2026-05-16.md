## 🎯 Batch 1 — First Round Ideas

### A. 🗡️ "Rival" — AI Debate Arena
**Track:** Surprise Us!  
Two AI personas argue any topic from opposing sides while you watch, vote, and learn. Pick *"Is crypto the future?"* and watch AI economists go head-to-head with real-time fact cards, argument strength meters, and a final verdict. Educational + addictive.

- **Core features:** Topic input → AI persona assignment → live debate → fact cards → strength meter → verdict
- **Why it wins:** Nothing like it exists. Viral potential. Judges can't stop clicking.
- **Difficulty:** Medium

---

### B. 🚀 "LaunchPad" — One-Sentence Startup Generator
**Track:** Business & E-commerce  
Type one sentence: *"Uber for dog walking."* AI generates a full startup kit — business model canvas, competitor map, MVP feature list, 90-day launch plan, revenue projections, and even a cold outreach email template. Interactive dashboard to iterate on each section.

- **Core features:** Idea input → BMC generator → competitor radar → MVP roadmap → email templates
- **Why it wins:** Every judge has a startup idea. They'll try it themselves immediately.
- **Difficulty:** Medium

---

### C. 🧠 "CaseBreaker" — AI Interview Case Simulator
**Track:** Learning & Education  
Simulates McKinsey/Google/FAANG-style case interviews with an AI interviewer. Adaptive difficulty — the AI pushes back on weak answers, gives hints when stuck, and scores your performance with detailed feedback. Tracks progress across sessions.

- **Core features:** Case selection → AI interviewer → adaptive Q&A → performance scoring → progress tracker
- **Why it wins:** Massive target audience. Real utility. Deep domain expertise available.
- **Difficulty:** Medium-High

---

### D. 🎮 "QuestForge" — AI Escape Room Generator
**Track:** Lifestyle & Game  
AI generates procedural escape rooms with interconnected puzzles. Each room has a theme (haunted lab, space station, heist vault), clues hidden in documents/images, and a countdown timer. AI hints system adapts to your progress.

- **Core features:** Theme picker → AI puzzle generator → clue system → timer → adaptive hints
- **Why it wins:** Games demo incredibly well. Replayable = sticky. Strong "Surprise" factor.
- **Difficulty:** High

---

## 🔥 Batch 2 — Second Round Ideas

### E. 😈 "Roast My ___" — Brutally Honest AI Feedback Studio
**Track:** Surprise Us!  
Submit your resume, startup pitch, dating profile, portfolio, or code snippet. Pick a feedback persona — **The VC** (cold, numbers-driven), **The Professor** (methodical, nitpicky), **The Brutally Honest Friend** (no filter, funny). Get scored, roasted, and rebuilt with actionable fixes. Share your roast score socially.

- **Core features:** Content upload → persona selector → roast generation → score card → social share
- **Why it wins:** Funny + useful = viral. Judges WILL paste their own resume in.
- **Difficulty:** Low-Medium

---

### F. 🌍 "StreetSmart" — Anti-Tourist City Intelligence
**Track:** Lifestyle  
Pick any city. AI generates a **local's survival guide** — hidden gems, scam alerts, cultural do's/don'ts, budget hacks, neighborhood safety vibes, best food by time-of-day, and a custom itinerary based on your vibe (foodie, nightlife, history nerd, solo traveler).

- **Core features:** City input → vibe quiz → hidden gems map → scam alerts → custom itinerary
- **Why it wins:** Everyone travels. Practical, personal, and endlessly replayable.
- **Difficulty:** Low-Medium

---

### G. 🧬 "LifeStack" — Personal Correlation Engine
**Track:** Work & Productivity  
Log your daily habits (sleep, meals, exercise, coffee, screen time, mood). AI runs correlation analysis: *"You're 73% more productive on days you sleep 7+ hrs and skip afternoon coffee."* Weekly insight reports + personalized experiments.

- **Core features:** Habit logger → correlation engine → insight reports → experiment suggestions → streak tracker
- **Why it wins:** Quantified-self is hot. Personal data + AI insights = addictive loop.
- **Difficulty:** Medium-High

---

### H. ⚔️ "Founders vs. AI Sharks" — Interactive Pitch Simulation
**Track:** Business & E-commerce  
You pitch your idea. **5 AI Sharks** (The Skeptic, The Numbers Guy, The Visionary, The Operator, The Wildcard) grill you with tough questions in real-time. They make offers, counter-offer each other, or walk out. You negotiate. Get a final investor-readiness scorecard.

- **Core features:** Pitch input → 5-shark Q&A → offer/negotiation engine → scorecard
- **Why it wins:** It's Shark Tank meets AI. Incredible to demo. Dramatic tension built in.
- **Difficulty:** Medium

---

### I. 🎵 "MoodForge" — Emotion-to-Everything Generator
**Track:** Surprise Us!  
Describe a **vibe** in natural language: *"rainy Tokyo midnight, neon reflections, melancholy but hopeful."* AI generates a matching color palette, playlist description, interior design mood board, outfit suggestion, writing prompt, and a cocktail recipe.

- **Core features:** Vibe input → multi-domain generator → mood board → shareable card
- **Why it wins:** Aesthetic, artistic, shareable. Nothing remotely like it exists.
- **Difficulty:** Low-Medium

## 🌀 Batch 3 — Third Round Ideas

### J. 🕰️ "TimeCapsule AI" — Messages to Your Future Self
**Track:** Lifestyle  
Write a letter, record a voice note, or upload a photo. Set a delivery date — 1 month, 1 year, 10 years. AI enriches it with world context from today (headlines, prices, trends), asks you to make predictions, and generates a sealed "time vault." Friends can add to each other's capsules.

- **Core features:** Content creator → delivery date picker → AI context enrichment → vault seal → delivery scheduler → friend sharing
- **Why it wins:** Emotionally powerful. Guaranteed demo impact. Zero competition.
- **Difficulty:** Medium

---

### K. 🧑‍⚖️ "Verdict" — AI Small Claims Court
**Track:** Surprise Us!  
Two people submit their side of a dispute — roommate drama, freelance payment gone wrong, who owes who at dinner. AI acts as judge: weighs both arguments, asks clarifying questions, cites logic, and delivers a legally-flavored verdict with reasoning. Shareable.

- **Core features:** Dual submission → AI clarification Q&A → argument weighing → verdict generation → shareable ruling card
- **Why it wins:** Relatable conflicts + dramatic verdicts = instant social sharing.
- **Difficulty:** Low-Medium

---

### L. 📰 "Unspun" — Bias-Free News Reconstructor
**Track:** Work & Productivity  
Paste any news article. AI strips political framing, identifies loaded language, shows the raw verified facts only, then reconstructs the story from 3 perspectives: Left, Right, and Neutral. Side-by-side comparison with bias score for the original.

- **Core features:** Article paste/URL → bias detection → fact extraction → 3-perspective reconstruction → bias score
- **Why it wins:** Deeply relevant in 2026. Every journalist and political junkie will use it.
- **Difficulty:** Medium

---

### M. 🧪 "What Would Happen If..." — Counterfactual History Engine
**Track:** Learning & Education  
Ask: *"What if the internet was invented in 1945?"* AI builds a full alternative timeline — political, economic, technological ripple effects — presented as a scrollable interactive documentary with branching decisions you can change.

- **Core features:** What-if prompt → timeline generator → ripple effect mapper → branching decisions → scrollable narrative
- **Why it wins:** Intellectually addictive. Endlessly replayable. Broad audience appeal.
- **Difficulty:** Medium-High

---

### N. 💼 "Shadow Resume" — What Your Resume Actually Says
**Track:** Work & Productivity  
Upload your resume. AI reveals what a recruiter sees in 6 seconds, what an ATS robot filters, what a hiring manager fears, and what a jealous colleague notices. Four brutally honest lenses, keyword gap analysis, rewrite suggestions, and a hireability score.

- **Core features:** Resume upload → 4-lens analysis → ATS score → keyword gap → rewrite suggestions → hireability score
- **Why it wins:** Every professional needs this. Immediate, personal value. Judges try it on themselves.
- **Difficulty:** Low-Medium

---

### O. 🌀 "Rabbit Hole" — Curated Obsession Generator
**Track:** Lifestyle  
Pick any topic: *"I want to understand black holes"* or *"I'm curious about the Medici family."* AI builds a **10-stop rabbit hole** — each stop has a concept, a stunning fact, a must-watch recommendation, a must-read link, and a cliffhanger that pulls you to the next stop. Like Netflix episode structure for learning.

- **Core features:** Topic input → 10-stop journey generator → fact cards → media links → cliffhanger hooks → progress tracker
- **Why it wins:** Perfectly timed for the attention-economy era. Insanely fun to experience.
- **Difficulty:** Low-Medium
